A third generation family enterprise

Shree Radhe Rani International Trading was founded nearly five decades ago by Late Shri Kunji Lal Agrawal, starting as a brokerage firm that grew into a prominent import and trading business, pioneering in agri-business.

Initially focused on wheat, maize, and paddy trading, the company diversified under Mr. Arun Kumar Singhania's leadership, expanding imports globally and venturing into rice and spices.

Mr. Gaurav Agrawal further broadened operations, moving into agri-processing and adding oils and food-grade chemicals to the portfolio.

Today, this third-generation family enterprise is a market leader in importing, processing, and distributing agri-commodities.
